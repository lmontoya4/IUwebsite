Why join?

By becoming a National member you will gain access to scholarships, life changing conferences, and networking opportunities with over 10,000 members nation wide! You gain the opportunity to upload your resume onto your account which is then available to professionals around the world! You can look up specific job/internship postings using the filter process to fit your personal interests You gain the opportunity to attend conferences and career fairs with dream companies as the picture above shows Welcome!

instructions

  1. Click the Join now button and create a new account
  2. Under "Related Links" follow the "Membership Application" Click "New Membership Application"
  3. Select your current title
  4. Click the Region 1 area on the map until you see a drop down menu appear under the map
  5. Select your chapter from Region 1: Scroll to the last option (University of California Merced)
  6. Fill out the Contact, Information, and Release forms, Do not forget to check the box confirming your awareness of the SHPE code of Ethics
  7. Select your payment method and fill out your information
  8. Hit the Submition Application button at the bottom
  9. Welcome to a network of high caliber students and professionals, or simply: La Familia
