Who are we?

Mission Statement

Ingenieros Unidos is a student chapter of the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE) at the University of California, Merced. Our vision as a chapter is to empower and develop the next generation of leaders in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). At IU, we provide our members with the opportunity to develop into the leading engineers of tomorrow. We have created an environment where members are encouraged to be innovators by instilling in them a desire to never stop growing. Through strategic methods of mentorship, empowerment, and exposure, we challenge our members to become the best they can be.

Vision

Ingenieros Unidos is committed to impacting the world by creating leading engineers, mathematicians, and scientists that are capable of: adapting to changing environments, posses visionary mindsets, and value growth and impact within the Hispanic Community. Aligned with the vision of SHPE: we envision a world where Hispanics are highly valued and known as leaders in their respective fields. IU is an organization founded on the SHPE familia principle, and recognizes the power behind being a support system for all our members. Driven by the desire to bridge the gap of Hispanics in STEM, we strive to create a pipeline within the Hispanic community through inspiration and guidance of the next generation and by pushing students of all ages to pursue higher education.
